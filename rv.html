Ragi, also known as Finger Millet, is a superfood packed with nutrients and has a unique earthy flavor.

It's gluten-free and high in protein, fiber, and essential vitamins and minerals. Not only is it tasty, but it's also a great way to satisfy your cravings without the guilt of indulging in sugary or fatty snacks.

Making your own Ragi Baked Snacks is super easy. Here's how:

1. Mix 1 cup of Ragi flour with 1/2 cup of Rice flour and 1/4 cup of Jowar

2. Add teaspoon of salt and 1/4 teaspoon of Sesame Seeds

3. Boil 1 cup of water and add all spices (turmeric, chilly powder black pepper poweder and salt).

4. Knead to form a dough ball, then divide it into small portions and make shapes.

5. Then bake them for about 5-7 minutes at 350°F (176°C).

6. Let them cool down completely before storing in an air-tight container.

7. Enjoy your homemade and nutritious Ragi Baked Snacks!
